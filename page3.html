Artist Statement

In my works, I used audacity to glitch my photos and combine my voice, photoshop to make my posters and premiere pro to edit my video. I also worked with my teammates to complete our net art with code and made a small game with p5.js. All these works I made are based on the projects of the class, but each one is my own subjective opinion. In my glitch art, I wanted to use this photo as a starting point for my DMA, because it was the first digital media art I had ever been exposed to. In the sound work, I chose my own recorded sound to synthesize a brand new sound source. My poster is intended to express my views on the proliferation of guns in America. In net art and mini-games, my teammates and I wanted to express our different experiences of social software and life in different ways.
In our p5js project, our inspiration comes from Pokemon and Mario games. We hope our audience can participate in our games, so we want to make a combined game with what we have learned. In this project, we found available image resources on the web and resized them in Photoshop. I was responsible for finding available sound sources on freesound and exporting them using the convert knowledge I learned in the sound project. Since we didn't know how to add sound to p5js, we looked it up online. In this project, my teammates and I completed all the work and experiments respectively.
